✦ Our Story

Art Passed Down
Through Generations

Born into a lineage of master henna artisans, Zara has spent over a decade perfecting the delicate dance between tradition and contemporary elegance. Each design is a meditation — a moment of pure creative devotion for your most sacred celebration.

2024
Global Artistry
